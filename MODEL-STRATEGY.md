## Cost Optimization

### **Expected Usage:**
- **CEO:** ~20-50 requests/day (high-value decisions)
- **Specialists:** ~100-500 requests/day total (operational tasks)

### **Monthly Cost Estimate:**
- **CEO (Opus):** ~$30-75/month
- **Specialists (Sonnet):** ~$15-40/month  
- **Total:** ~$45-115/month for AI reasoning

### **Efficiency Gains:**
- **CEO handles complex routing** → Reduces need for human oversight
- **Specialists handle routine tasks** → Scales team capacity
- **Smart delegation** → Right agent, first time
